# Lab 06 Case Study Brief

## Case: Peer Tutoring Booking Platform

University students frequently struggle to find reliable peer tutors, especially during exam periods when the need is most urgent. Current alternatives include asking friends, posting in class chat groups, or waiting for word-of-mouth recommendations — methods that are slow, unreliable, and often fail right when help is needed most. Willing tutors exist but lack visibility, and students often settle for a tutor they aren't confident in due to the informal, unstructured search process.

## Target Users

- Student who needs academic help and wants to find a suitable tutor quickly
- Student who is willing to tutor others but currently has no way to be discovered
- Admin/coordinator who verifies tutor listings and updates booking status

## Product Direction

A web-based prototype that allows students to search for tutors by subject, view tutor profiles and availability, submit a booking request, and track booking status. Admins can review and update booking status and manage tutor listings, replacing informal chat-based coordination with a structured, centralized system.
