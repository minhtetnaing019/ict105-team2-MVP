# Success Metrics and Decision Rules

## 1. Success Metrics

The following metrics will be used to evaluate whether the MVP prototype successfully meets user needs during usability testing.

| Metric ID | Success Metric | Target | Measurement Method | Related Requirement |
|-----------|----------------|--------|--------------------|---------------------|
| M-01 | Task Completion Rate | 80% or higher | Number of participants who complete all assigned tasks ÷ Total participants | FR-01, FR-03, FR-06, FR-07, FR-08 |
| M-02 | Form Completion Accuracy | 80% or higher | Number of valid booking submissions ÷ Total booking attempts | FR-03, FR-10 |
| M-03 | Tutor Search Success Rate | 80% or higher | Number of participants who successfully find the requested tutor ÷ Total participants | FR-06 |
| M-04 | Booking Confirmation Success | 100% | Booking confirmation message appears after every valid submission | FR-11 |
| M-05 | Booking Status Visibility | 80% or higher | Participants successfully locate their booking status on the dashboard | FR-08 |
| M-06 | Overall Usability Rating | Average 4.0 / 5.0 or higher | Participant feedback form | FR-01 to FR-11 |
| M-07 | Feature Usefulness Rating | Average 4.0 / 5.0 or higher | Participant feedback form | MVP Features |

---

## 2. Decision Rules

The project team will make decisions based on the experiment results.

| Experiment Result | Decision |
|-------------------|----------|
| All or most success metrics meet the target | Continue developing the final prototype with the current design. |
| One or two metrics do not meet the target | Improve the affected screens, labels, navigation, or form design before implementation. |
| Several metrics fail to meet the target | Review the user stories, functional requirements, and interface design before continuing development. |
| Participants cannot complete the main booking workflow | Re-evaluate the MVP scope and redesign the booking process before implementation. |

---

## 3. Expected Outcome

If the majority of participants can successfully search for a tutor, view tutor details, submit a booking request, receive a confirmation message, and check their booking status with minimal confusion, the MVP will be considered successful and ready for further development.
